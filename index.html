Artique — Core Brand

Custom fashion designed

Artique is a custom fashion house for women who see clothing as more than fabric. We design pieces that honour your silhouette, your culture, and your story — no two garments alike.

From bridal lehengas to everyday abayas, from festive Indo-Arabic fusion to clean tailored sets — every design begins with a conversation and ends with something unmistakably yours.

How It Works

Your design journey,
from first meeting to final fitting.

Artique is entirely consultation-based. Every piece goes through a deliberate, personal process.

💬
01
Consultation

One-on-one meeting to understand your vision, occasion, body, and preferences. Virtual or in-boutique.

✏️
02
Design & Fabric

Our designer sketches your look. You select fabrics, embellishments, and silhouette from our curated collection.

🪡
03
Tailoring

Expert craftsmanship. Your garment is hand-crafted to your exact measurements with precision and care.

✨
04
Final Fitting

Try it. Adjust it. Own it. We don't deliver until it's exactly right — not for us, but for you.

Our Story

Born from a belief
that design is a language.

Artique began as a simple idea in Abu Dhabi: that fashion doesn't have to choose between Indian roots and Arabic elegance. That a woman can wear a lehenga with the same intentionality she hangs a painting on her wall.

We built a creative house — not just a boutique. A space where craftsmanship, personalization, and cultural fusion come together in every single piece we make.

Craftsmanship
Every stitch made with intention and skill, never rushed.
Personalization
We design around your body, culture, and story.
Cultural Fusion
South Asian heritage meets the refined Gulf aesthetic.
